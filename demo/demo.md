# Farouk — Demo Script

This document describes the demo video / GIF that should accompany the repository.
Use it to record a screen capture or produce a product video.

---

## Overview

**Duration:** 90–120 seconds
**Format:** Screen recording with cursor highlights + optional voiceover
**Goal:** Show the full Farouk loop — idea in, startup out — in under 2 minutes

---

## Scene-by-Scene Breakdown

---

### Scene 1 — The blank slate (0:00–0:08)

**What to show:**
The Farouk interface loads. Clean, minimal. The input field is empty.
The sidebar shows: Launch · Strategy · CEO Plan · Agents · Tasks · Outputs · Log.

**Voiceover (optional):**
> "You have an idea. Farouk has everything else."

**Why this scene matters:**
First impression = simplicity. The user must feel: "I can use this."

---

### Scene 2 — The idea is typed (0:08–0:18)

**What to show:**
Cursor moves to the input field. Text appears character by character (simulate typing):

```
Launch a coaching business for young professionals
```

The "Launch OS" button is clicked.

**Voiceover (optional):**
> "One sentence. That's all Farouk needs."

---

### Scene 3 — Strategy streams live (0:18–0:38)

**What to show:**
The interface switches to the **Strategy** tab.
Text streams in real time — McKinsey-style analysis:
- Market opportunity
- Target users
- Positioning
- Business model

The cursor blinks as tokens arrive. The reader can see real, specific insights forming.

**Voiceover (optional):**
> "Farouk thinks like a consultant. Market size, positioning, business model — instantly."

**Why this scene matters:**
This is the first "wow" moment. Real strategy, not templates.

---

### Scene 4 — The CEO takes over (0:38–0:52)

**What to show:**
Auto-switch to the **CEO Plan** tab.
A 90-day roadmap appears, streaming:
- Phase 1: Foundation
- Phase 2: Build and test
- Phase 3: Launch

Agent assignments appear with specific missions.

**Voiceover (optional):**
> "Then Farouk becomes the CEO. It builds the roadmap and assembles the team."

---

### Scene 5 — The agents are dispatched (0:52–1:10)

**What to show:**
The **Agents** tab. Four agent cards appear:
- Léa (Marketing) — status changes from "idle" to "working..."
- Hakim (Engineering) — same
- Sarah (Sales) — same
- Omar (Ops) — same

Progress bars fill. The dispatch log shows messages from each agent as they complete their tasks. Tasks in the Tasks tab tick off one by one with green checkmarks.

**Voiceover (optional):**
> "Four AI employees. Running in parallel. No meetings required."

**Why this scene matters:**
This is the emotional peak. It *feels* like a real team working.

---

### Scene 6 — The deliverables arrive (1:10–1:30)

**What to show:**
The **Outputs** tab. A full startup report streams in:
- Executive Summary
- Go-to-Market Strategy
- Product Roadmap
- Revenue Projections
- Week 1 Action Items

Scroll slowly through the output. Let the viewer read real content.

**Voiceover (optional):**
> "Business plan. GTM strategy. Revenue projections. Week 1 actions. Done."

---

### Scene 7 — Language switch (1:30–1:40)

**What to show:**
Click the `EN → FR` toggle in the header.
The entire interface switches to French instantly.
Show the input field, agents tab, and a French output block.

**Voiceover (optional):**
> "Farouk parle aussi français."

---

### Scene 8 — The close (1:40–1:50)

**What to show:**
Pull back to show the full interface. All tabs populated. Activity log filled with timestamps.
End on the Farouk logo + tagline:

```
Farouk — AI CEO that builds and runs startups
```

**Voiceover (optional):**
> "Your idea. Your team. Your startup. Open-source, free forever."

---

## Recording Notes

- Use a 1440×900 or 1280×800 window
- Disable notifications before recording
- Use smooth cursor movements (consider a cursor highlighter tool)
- Record at 60fps for smooth streaming text
- Export as:
  - `.gif` (max 10MB) for the README embed
  - `.mp4` (full quality) for YouTube / Product Hunt
